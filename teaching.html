MIT 6.862: Applied Machine Learning

In the Fall of 2020 and Spring of 2021, I served as a Teaching Assistant for MIT's graduate applied machine learning course.

Alongside Prof. Iddo Drori, The Applied Machine Learning course is an offering for non-EECS graduate students at MIT Looking to apply machine learning to their research or thesis. Students complete a semester-long project in addition to introductory coursework and labs.
