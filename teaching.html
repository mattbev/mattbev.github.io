Courses

MIT 6.862: Applied Machine Learning

In the Fall of 2020 and Spring of 2021, I served as a Teaching Assistant for MIT's graduate applied machine learning course.

Alongside Prof. Iddo Drori, the Applied Machine Learning course is an offering for non-EECS graduate students at MIT Looking to apply machine learning to their research or thesis. Students complete a semester-long project in addition to introductory coursework and labs. Approximately 50 students were enrolled in both semesters.

MIT 6.036: Introduction to Machine Learning

In the Fall of 2020, I served as a Teaching Assistant for MIT's undergraduate machine learning course.

Along with my responsibilities for the graduate Applied Machine Learning course, I also was a member of the teaching staff for the undergraduate course. For this, I ran labs, held office hours, and helped design course materials. Approximately 500 students were enrolled.
